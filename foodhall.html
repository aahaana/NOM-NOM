ABOUT
Foodhall
"Food is the ingredient that binds us together!"

FoodHall is Nom Nom's heartfelt attempt to give culinary expression the podium it deserves. Here, from all the innovative Food Photography, Food Art and original Recipes participants submit, each of every category that stands out to be the most extra-ordinary will hold the stage for a day, as concluded by an esteemed panel of Michelin-starred Chefs, globally-known Photographers and Artists. The selections of the day will be featured here on the website and on all our social media handles. Check out today's selections for inspiration.
