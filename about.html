What We Believe:
Our relationship with food, as a species, has exceeded way above survival. Food comforts us, it brings us together, it heals us, it completes us. We, at Nom Nom, believe that there's a foodie in all of us, whose eye adorns a sparkle everytime a bite touches our heart while our mouth goes 'nom-nom'! We strive to provide a platform that brings everyone together to learn, explore and express with food. We yearn to revive the fading science of food to help heal our relationship with it in our 'Food Science' section. We want you to explore the culmination of flavours and cultures whenever your heart asks for it in the 'Restaurants' section. And, we want you to intrepidly express your inner-master of culinary, artisitic and visual skills in the 'FoodHall'. We hope to welcome you into our extensive family of gut-touching abundance! See you around.
